About Me

Hi, I’m Jacek Flores, a passionate 18 year old photographer who loves capturing both nature's beauty and people's stories. I specialize in both landscape and portrait photography but am always open to trying new styles. I'm always seeking new projects and exciting opportunities to tell stories through my lens.

Photography started as a hobby, but it has quickly turned into a passion. I actively search for locations or just bring the camera to capture whatever I can. Whether you're looking for a photographer for an event, portraits, or simply to collaborate, I’m open to talk.
